Convenience

No need to worry about drop-offs and pick-ups. All you need to do is schedule your weekly slot and I will come straight to your door for your piano lesson. Busy parents can simply make arrangements with their child provider for the weekly lesson. If you prefer, I also offer the option of hosting the lesson at my studio in Syosset.


Track Record

15 years of proven results, including top grades at NYSSMA every year. My students are able to user their accomplishments to give an extra boost to their applications to the most selective universities in the country. Students also have the option to prepare for exams at the Royal Conservatory of Music or prepare for recitals in local concert venues.


Customization

Each student will be given a unique lesson plan developed according to their amibitions, time commitment, and skill level. Whether the end goal is entrance to a music academy or simply performing a Christmas song for their family, I make sure that each student gets the results they want. No matter what their level, all students regularly have a chance to showcase their work at regular recitals.

Teacher Bio

Sophia Guiza has spent most of her life immersed in piano and received her formal music education and masters degree in Moscow, Russia. She has over 30 years of experience teaching piano to students from all ages and backgrounds. In her free time, she expands her skillset by attending specialized workshops in music education and following the latest developments in the industry. She is listed as an accredited teacher by the Royal Conservatory of Music and is also a certified Steinway Educational Partner. Her students have received top scores at NYSSMA assessments, passed exams at the Royal Conservatory of Music, and have been accepted to the Manhattan School of Music.
